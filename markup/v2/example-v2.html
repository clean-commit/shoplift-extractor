Pausing a test

Pausing a test will return visitors on the variant to the original experience while the test remains paused. As this could affect the validity of your data, pausing a test is only recommended when absolutely necessary. For example, if you need to make extensive edits to both your original and variant.

Editing your variant could affect the validity of your data

Please be aware that editing the variant on a live test could affect the validity of your test data. The effect on data validity will depend on how long your test has been live, as well as the types of changes you plan to make. Please proceed with caution.
